— Good to Know

Questions, answered.

Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?+
We run by appointment so every chair gets its full, unhurried time — book online in under a minute. If we have a same-day opening it'll show up in the booking calendar, but reserving ahead is the surest way in.
How long does a sitting take?+
Anywhere from 30 minutes for beard work to 90 for the Full Sitting. Each service lists its time when you book, and we block the room accordingly so you're never rushed.
What's your cancellation policy?+
Life happens — just give us at least 24 hours' notice to reschedule free of charge. You can cancel any upcoming sitting yourself from the list on the Book page.
Can I request a specific barber?+
Absolutely. Pick your barber in step two of booking, or tap "Book [name]" on any profile on the Barbers page. Prefer to leave it to us? Choose "Whoever's free" for the first available chair.
How do I pay?+
Pay in the chair by card or cash — no deposit required to book. Prices shown are final; tipping is appreciated but never expected.
Do you sell gift cards?+
We do — they make a sharp gift. Drop us a note through the form below and we'll set one up in any amount.
